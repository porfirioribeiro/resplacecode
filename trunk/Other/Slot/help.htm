 Introduction

 Slots MAX is a simulation of a mechanical slot machine with three wheels and two deals. The second deal, if enabled, does not charge your credit. Statistics for both game deals, several controls for credit and bet, auto-hold, sound and voice effects are a few of this simulator's features. Enjoy it and good luck!

 How to play

First of all, you have to choose the game bet (between 1 and 10) and 'insert' 1000 coins by pressing the 'COIN' button. The handler placed in the bottom right corner will spin all three wheels for the first deal and the not-held ones for the second deal. The current line winning is displayed on the left and is added to your credit each time you start a new deal. Your current credit could be cash out with 'O' button and your account status is available in Settings page. When your credit or your account reaches a very high value, it will be automatically adjusted to the default one. This initialization can be done with the reset button (near account field), which will erase also the statistics table. When the second hand option is enabled, you can change the held wheel with a mouse click or with key 1, 2 or 3.

These keyboard shortcuts are available:

H - this help file;

X - exit game;

1,2,3 - held the appropriate wheels;

Space or Enter - spin the wheels!

C - insert coins;

O or  0 - cash out;

S or T - settings page

A - auto play;

< - decreases the bet; (same as mouse wheel)

> - increases the bet;

 

 Payout table

The prizes of this game are listed in the payout table. Each number from the right is multiplied with the current bet to compute the line prize. When the first or second deal is finished, this prize will be added to your credit.
